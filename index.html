Biography

Arijit Singh is a renowned Indian playback singer who has achieved great fame and recognition for his soulful and melodious voice. He was born on April 25, 1987, in Jiaganj, Murshidabad, West Bengal, India. From a young age, Arijit displayed a keen interest in music and started his musical journey by participating in various reality singing competitions. His breakthrough came in 2005 when he participated in the reality show "Fame Gurukul" and secured the sixth position. Although he didn't win the show, it opened up doors for him in the music industry. Arijit's career as a playback singer began in 2010 when he got the opportunity to sing the song "Phir Mohabbat" for the movie "Murder 2." Despite being a relatively unknown singer at the time, his soulful rendition of the song garnered widespread praise and marked the beginning of his success story. In 2013, Arijit gained immense popularity with the song "Tum Hi Ho" from the film "Aashiqui 2." The song became a massive hit and earned him numerous awards, including the Filmfare Award for Best Male Playback Singer. Following this breakthrough, Arijit became one of the most sought-after singers in the Indian film industry. Over the years, Arijit Singh has lent his voice to numerous Bollywood movies and delivered several chart-topping hits. Some of his notable songs include "Kabira" from "Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani," "Ae Dil Hai Mushkil" from the film of the same name, "Channa Mereya" from "Ae Dil Hai Mushkil," "Tera Ban Jaunga" from "Kabir Singh," and "Kalank" from the film "Kalank," among many others. Arijit's voice is known for its versatility and emotional depth, allowing him to excel in various genres, including romantic ballads, soulful melodies, and peppy tracks. He has collaborated with several music composers and directors, establishing himself as the go-to singer for capturing the emotions of a song. Apart from his playback singing career, Arijit has also released several non-film singles, including "Phir Bhi Tumko Chaahunga" and "Tera Yaar Hoon Main." His independent work has also garnered acclaim from both critics and fans. Arijit Singh's contributions to the music industry have been recognized with numerous awards and accolades. He has received several Filmfare Awards, IIFA Awards, and Zee Cine Awards, among others, for his outstanding vocal performances. Arijit Singh continues to enthrall music lovers with his soul-stirring voice and remains one of the most popular and beloved singers in India. His ability to convey emotions through his singing has earned him a special place in the hearts of millions of fans worldwide.
